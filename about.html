About GoParcel

GoParcel is an online booking platform for parcel delivery service worldwide. We automatically choose the best delivery solution for you, which means that you get the best service for the lowest available price. We work closely with the best courier partners across the world to bring you first class affordable door-to-door parcel delivery services within Ireland and to any destination worldwide. Further to this, GoParcel is not a comparison website, meaning that delivery should be simple and fast. With GoParcel the booking process requires only just few clicks. Our technology driven solution introduces a more organized, user-friendly and cost-effective approach to delivery. We grant access to high quality delivery services for customers who want to send parcel to any destination.

Our Main Advantage Are:

  • Technology
  • Competitive prices
  • Security
